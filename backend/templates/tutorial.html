How to Use AccessBank

Getting Started with Chat

Start a Chat

Click the "Start Chat" button on the homepage or use voice command "start chat" to begin your conversation with our AI assistant.

Type Your Message

Use the text input field to type your questions or requests. Press Enter or click Send to submit.

Voice Commands

Click the microphone icon or say "enable voice" to use voice commands for hands-free interaction.

Accessibility Features

High Contrast Mode

Say "enable high contrast" or use the accessibility settings to toggle high contrast mode for better visibility.

Screen Reader Support

Enable screen reader optimization for improved compatibility with assistive technologies.

Text Size Adjustment

Use voice commands "increase text size" or "decrease text size" to adjust text size, or use the slider in accessibility settings.

Voice Commands

Navigate the entire application using voice commands like "go to home", "start chat", "enable high contrast", and more.

Language Support

Select Language

Choose your preferred language from the dropdown menu for translated responses.

Translate Chat

Click the translate button to convert messages to your selected language in real-time.
